Oven Baked Chicken Teriyaki Recipe

Description

Wonderful baked chicken with minimal mess to make for easy clean-up. My daughter says it's her favorite teriyaki chicken ever! Can be easily adapted to your tastes and can also be done on the stove if you prefer. Serve over rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup mirin (Japanese sweet wine)
  • 4 teaspoons grated fresh ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Whisk cornstarch & water in small bowl. Combine soy sauce, sugar, mirin, ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes in saucepan over medium heat until mixture starts to simmer. Whisk in cornstarch mixture. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until thickened, stirring often, about 10 minutes.
  3. Pat chicken dry w/ paper towels; arrange in baking dish. Pour sauce over chicken breasts, coating all sides.
  4. Bake chicken breasts in preheated oven until no longer pink in center and juices run clear, 25 to 30 minutes.
