Jollof Rice

Recipe Description

The most popular dish in West Africa, Jollof Rice is a wonderful dish made with a nice blend of Tomato Sauce with Rice and can even be served with some Grilled Beef (Suya).

1/2 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper, 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric, 2 and a 4th cup of Basmati rice, 1 bay leaf, 1 tablespoon of chicken bouillon paste and an optional chopped cilantro for garnish.
